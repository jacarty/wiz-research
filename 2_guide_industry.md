# Industry Strategy Map Generator - User Guide

## What It Does

Generates comprehensive, executive-level industry intelligence reports in a scannable text format. Perfect for strategic research, competitive intelligence, and market analysis.

**Key Features:**
- Industry overview with market dynamics and growth projections
- Revenue drivers and strategic opportunities
- Challenges, risks, and competitive landscape
- Regulatory and compliance requirements
- **Deep-dive on technology innovation** (cloud, SaaS, AI applications)
- Curated sources with verification requirements
- Executive-ready format (8-12 pages)

---

## How to Use

### Basic Structure

```
Create an executive-level Industry Strategy Map for the [INDUSTRY] industry and [SEGMENT].

[Paste the full prompt template]
```

### Example Inputs

**Geographic + Sector:**
```
UK Financial Services
European Manufacturing
US Healthcare Technology
Global SaaS
```

**With Specific Focus:**
```
UK Financial Services - Focus on wealth management and digital banking transformation
US Healthcare - Emphasize mid-market hospital systems
European E-commerce - Compare Western Europe vs CEE markets
```

**With Company Context:**
```
Global SaaS - Companies in $50M-$500M ARR range
UK Retail - Focus on omnichannel retailers with 50-500 stores
```

---

## What You'll Get

### 7 Core Sections:

1. **Industry Overview** - Market size, players, maturity stage
2. **Growth Areas & Revenue Drivers** - 4-6 key opportunities with sizing and timelines
3. **Strategic Challenges** - Operational, market, technology, and strategic risks
4. **Regulatory & Compliance** - Current frameworks, priorities, emerging regulations
5. **Technology Innovation** - **Most detailed section:**
   - Cloud adoption strategies and use cases
   - SaaS platform economics
   - AI/ML applications by business function
   - Emerging technologies
   - Investment priorities
6. **Strategic Imperatives** - Success factors, competitive dynamics, partnerships
7. **Sources & References** - 10-15 categorized, authoritative sources

---

## Key Differentiators

### Verification Requirements (New in v2.0)
- All data points **must be sourced** with inline citations
- Only real, verifiable company examples
- Clear distinction between verified facts and industry observations
- Source quality hierarchy emphasized (regulatory bodies → analysts → trade press)

### Technology Section Depth
The report goes beyond surface-level tech descriptions:
- Explains **how** technologies drive revenue or reduce costs
- Provides specific, working use cases
- Quantifies impact where possible
- Identifies differentiators vs table stakes
- Shows integration patterns (cloud + SaaS + AI working together)

## Tips for Best Results

**1. Be Specific with Industry Definition**
- ✅ "UK Financial Services - retail banking and wealth management"
- ❌ "Banking"

**2. Add Context for Targeted Insights**
- "Focus on companies responding to [specific regulation]"
- "Emphasize sustainability initiatives"
- "Include cloud migration considerations"

**3. Request Depth on Key Areas**
- "Provide extra detail on AI compliance applications"
- "Expand multi-cloud vendor dynamics"
- "Include more SaaS integration examples"

**4. Iterate After Initial Output**
- "Add more quantitative market data"
- "Expand UK-specific regulatory requirements"
- "Include case studies for top 3 AI use cases"
- "Create a one-page executive summary"

## Output Format

- **Markdown text document** (can be converted to Word/PDF)
- Hierarchical headers for scannability
- Bullet points for key facts
- Inline citations for all data points
- 8-12 pages of dense, actionable content
- Executive-level detail (comprehensive but not academic)

---

## Quality Standards

**The prompt enforces:**
- Verified, factual information only
- Source attribution for all claims
- Real examples (no speculation)
- Current regulatory information
- Transparency about uncertainty
- Prioritization of primary sources

**Avoids:**
- Generic platitudes
- Unsourced statistics
- Outdated information
- Speculative predictions
- Anecdotal claims without documentation
