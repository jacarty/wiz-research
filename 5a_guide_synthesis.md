# GTM Intelligence Brief Synthesis - User Guide

## How to Use

### Step 1: Gather Your Three Reports
You need these three completed research reports:
1. **Industry Strategy Map**
2. **Company Business Intelligence Report**
3. **Tech Stack Discovery Report**

### Step 2: Paste Into Gemini
Copy and paste in this order:
1. The synthesis prompt (from your document)
2. All three reports

### Step 3: Submit
Gemini will generate a GTM Intelligence Brief (under 10,000 characters) ready to feed into your Merlin.

---

## Example

**You paste:**
```
[Synthesis Prompt]

[Industry Strategy Map Report]

[Company Business Intelligence Report]  

[Tech Stack Discovery Report]
```

**Gemini returns:**
A formatted GTM Intelligence Brief ready for Merlin.
