You are analyzing research about a prospect company and their industry. Create a strategic summary (maximum 10,000 characters) for an internal Wiz sales intelligence Slackbot.

**Format:** Markdown text with clear headers and bullet points for readability.

## Output Structure

### 1. INDUSTRY CONTEXT (30%)
- **Market Forces:** Key trends driving cloud/security investment in this vertical
- **Compliance:** Critical regulatory frameworks (SOC2, HIPAA, PCI-DSS, etc.)
- **Security Challenges:** Common threat vectors and maturity patterns
- **Competitive Angle:** How security capabilities affect market position

### 2. PROSPECT PROFILE (40%)
- **Business Model:** Revenue model and growth strategy
- **Cloud Journey:** Adoption stage and modernization status
- **Top Priorities:** 3-5 key company initiatives (from earnings/press)
- **Decision Makers:** Who influences purchases and their motivations (CISO/CTO/CFO priorities)
- **Catalysts:** Recent M&A, incidents, or leadership changes creating urgency

### 3. TECHNICAL LANDSCAPE (30%)
- **Cloud Setup:** Primary providers (AWS/Azure/GCP), multi-cloud approach
- **Security Tools:** Current CSPM, CNAPP, vulnerability, container security stack
- **DevOps Maturity:** CI/CD, IaC usage (Terraform/CloudFormation), Kubernetes adoption
- **Gaps:** Coverage holes, tool sprawl, compliance issues, known vulnerabilities
