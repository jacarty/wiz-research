# User Guide: Discovery Call Prep (NotebookLM)

## How to Use

1. Upload your prospect research to NotebookLM
2. **Optional:** Tell NotebookLM who you're meeting with (e.g., "CISO and Cloud Security Manager")
3. Paste the prompt and run
4. NotebookLM generates MEDDPICC gap analysis and role-tailored questions

---

## What You'll Get

- **MEDDPICC Gap Analysis** - What we know vs. what's missing across all 8 elements
- **3 Question Funnels** - Tailored to your meeting attendees (open → probing → confirming)
- **Red Flags** - Deal risks and unvalidated assumptions

Questions are customized to the prospect's industry, tech stack, and the seniority/function of who's in the room.

---

## Output Use

Use this to:
- Identify critical unknowns before the call
- Prepare targeted discovery questions
- Avoid deal-blocking surprises
- Run more strategic conversations
