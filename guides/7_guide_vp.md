# User Guide: Strategic Value Pyramid (NotebookLM)

## How to Use

1. Upload prospect research documents to NotebookLM
2. Paste the prompt and run
3. NotebookLM generates an executive-ready strategic summary

---

## What You'll Get

A structured pyramid summary with:
- **Company Mission** - One-sentence business purpose
- **Supporting Strategy** - 2-3 points on tech/digital transformation approach
- **Key Initiatives** - 3-4 current projects (cloud, security, AI/ML, DevOps, compliance)
- **Risks & Challenges** - 3-4 critical obstacles framed as business impacts
- **Technical Context** - Cloud platforms, security tools, relevant tech stack

Written in business language for executive conversations, with urgency drivers highlighted.

---

## Output Use

Use this to:
- Prep for C-level meetings
- Brief your team on strategic context
- Build executive presentation decks
- Position solutions to business objectives
