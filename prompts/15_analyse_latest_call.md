# Call Summary & Action Report

Analyze the most recent call recording and extract actionable intelligence.

## Output Structure

### 1. CALL OVERVIEW
- Date, participants (name/title), purpose, duration, tone

### 2. KEY DISCUSSION POINTS
- Topics in order discussed
- Customer problems/challenges raised
- Customer questions (interest signals)
- Wiz capabilities discussed
- Current tools/situation mentioned

### 3. ATTENDEE ANALYSIS
Per speaker:
- Engagement: Active | Moderate | Passive
- Sentiment: Enthusiastic | Positive | Neutral | Skeptical | Resistant
- Key statements/questions
- Confidence: High/Medium/Low

### 4. BUYING SIGNALS & CONCERNS
**Positive:** Urgency, timeline, budget mentions; technical deep-dives; next steps from customer

**Concerns:** Hesitations, competitor mentions, blockers raised

### 5. COMMITTED NEXT STEPS
- Actions with owner/deadline
- Customer commitments
- Our commitments
- Follow-up meeting details

### 6. RECOMMENDED ACTIONS
- Immediate (24-48hrs)
- Research needed
- Stakeholders to engage
- Resources to prepare

### 7. ENGAGEMENT STRATEGY
Per key participant:
- Follow-up topic based on their comments
- 3 targeted questions for their priorities/concerns/influence

### 8. INFORMATION GAPS
- MEDDPICC gaps
- Technical requirements unknown
- Key stakeholders missing
- Competitive landscape unclear

## Standards
- Quote directly with timestamps when possible
- Mark "Inferred" vs stated facts
- Focus on what was said, not what should have been
- Flag uncovered qualification areas
