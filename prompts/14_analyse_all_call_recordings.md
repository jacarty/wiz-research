# Meeting Intelligence Report
Analyze all call recordings for this opportunity and generate actionable sales intelligence.

## Output Structure

### 1. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY (3-4 sentences)
- Meetings to date, purpose, and outcomes
- Overall sentiment and engagement trajectory
- Key decisions made or progression achieved

### 2. ATTENDEES & ENGAGEMENT
Per participant:
- Name, title, buying process role
- Stance: Champion | Supportive | Neutral | Skeptical | Blocker
- Key quotes/behaviors showing position
- Confidence: High/Medium/Low

### 3. BUSINESS INTELLIGENCE
- Pain points with context and severity
- Buying signals (urgency, budget, timeline)
- Technical requirements (integrations, compliance, features)
- Current tools/competitors and sentiment
- Success metrics/KPIs referenced

### 4. AGREED NEXT STEPS
- Committed actions with owners/deadlines
- Resources to provide
- Follow-ups scheduled/proposed

### 5. MEDDPICC GAP ANALYSIS
Learned vs. missing across: Metrics, Economic Buyer, Decision Criteria, Decision Process, Paper Process, Identify Pain, Champion, Competition

### 6. RECOMMENDED ACTIONS
Beyond agreed steps:
- Stakeholders to engage 1:1 (and why)
- Information gaps to research
- Internal resources to involve
- Risk mitigation steps

### 7. INDIVIDUAL FOLLOW-UP STRATEGY
Per key attendee:
- **Topics**: Areas to explore based on their comments
- **Questions**: 3-5 open-ended, broad to specific, to uncover needs/concerns/influence

### 8. RISK FLAGS
- Missing decision-makers
- Misaligned expectations/objections
- Timeline/budget/process concerns
- Competitive threats

## Standards
- Source claims with timestamps/quotes where possible
- Flag assumptions explicitly when confidence is low
- Prioritise actionable intelligence over generic observations
- Use UK business terminology
